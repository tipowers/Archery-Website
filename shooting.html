Shooting the Bow:

  • First and foremost....try out different bows with varying draw weights to see what poundage bow you should be shooting. You can KNOW technique, but STILL shoot poor if you are shaking trying to pull the bow back. It is best to start with a low draw weight and work your way up from there. 35 lbs is a good starting point. Watch the video below for more information or visit How To Choose the Right Draw Weight.
  • After you have determined your draw weight it is time to pick up a bow and start shooting! Watch the video below for more information or visit Tips On How To Shoot.
  • Now that you know the different bow types, the components of a recurve bow, and how to assemble (follow this link on Bow Assembly) and shoot your recurve bow it's time to watch me shoot a few arrows for fun below!
